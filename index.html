Green Hat CTF was a thrilling three-day cybersecurity event organized by the Micro Club at Dar Chabab. It brought together a large number of participants for a series of CTF challenges, workshops, and conferences. The event focused on solving cybersecurity problems in areas like cryptography and web security, with participants competing for the top spots. Alongside the competition, expert talks and hands-on activities provided valuable learning experiences. The event concluded with three standout winners and fostered a sense of community and growth in the field of cybersecurity.
